Dylan Howard

CEO



Growing up, I was raised by a sales family, my parents owned a car business in California and my father would always teach me the art of sales, people, and business. As I grew up, I learned from leading professionals the art of business in many different sectors. Having a problem with employee communication and productivity? Let's fix that. Is your marketing plan not working? Let's identify the problem (and not waste your money while we do it). Is your company not getting the PR it needs, don't worry, I've gotten media for multiple individuals and their brand and company on major publication.

Essentially it comes down to this, you can work with a PR or marketing firm and spend thousands of dollars just to be told "we don't guarantee it will work" or you can work with me and I will work until we get the results you want in whatever area you need (without changing the original price or agreement)! I will work and use all of my knowledge, every technique, and tool that I have to make sure you are successful. As an Eagle Scout, you have my word!

Who am I when I'm not working? I'm a husband and father as well as foodie. I love exploring new areas, trying new things (except at the drive-thru), I'm a wrestler and enjoy working out. However, I also love to give back to the community and help them come together, so I started a nonprofit (501(c)3 approved) called Unity Community Initiative.
